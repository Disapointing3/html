Van Gelder

Van Gelder is a world class artistic men's gymnastics still rings athlete.

He is known for his strength and clean form on rings.

According to Wikipedia, VG was born in Waalwijk, Netherlands. His list of accomplishments include: gold in men's rings at 2005 European Championships, world champion in men's rings at the 2005 World Artistic Gymnastics Championships, bronze at the 2006 World Artistic Gymnastics Championships, and silver at the 2007 World Artistic Gymnastics Championships.
