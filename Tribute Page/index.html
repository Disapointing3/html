Van Gelder

"The Lord of the Rings"

Van Gelder is a world class artistic men's gymnastics still rings athlete.

He is known for his strength and clean form on rings.
  • Born in Waalwijk, Netherlands,
  • His list of accomplishments include:
    • Gold in men's rings at 2005 European Championships
    • World champion in men's rings at the 2005 World Artistic Gymnastics Championships
    • Bronze at the 2006 World Artistic Gymnastics Championships
    • Silver at the 2007 World Artistic Gymnastics Championships.
  • He has a skill named after him called the Van Gelder (an E skill in CoP)
